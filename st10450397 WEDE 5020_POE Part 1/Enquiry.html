History

Slime Season Apparel is a street and trendy inspired clothing brand founded in 2022 by Ayahluma Oyiya. It was created as a bold and creative streetwear brand born from the urban culture of Johannesburg, South Africa. Inspired by the “slime” slang popularized in hip-hop culture, this brand represents loyalty, authenticity and staying to one’s true circle. Starting with a small range of printed t-shirts and hoodies, the brand quickly gained attention for its unique color palettes, bold graphics, and confident slogans. Social media and local pop-op events helped grow the following, attracting a loyal community of young trendsetters. Today, Slime Apparel continues to expand its collection, incorporating sustainable materials and limited edition drops, while staying true to its mission: drip with meaning, move with loyalty.

Mission

Slime Apparel is commited to create high quality, stylish streetwear that inspires self-expression, confidence, and loyalty. We are determined to blending bold urban culture with comfort and sustainability, giving our community clothing that tells their story.

Vission

Our Vission is to the become leading African streetwear brand recognized worldwide for authenticity, innovation, and cultural influence, while empowering the next generation of creatives and trendsetters.

Services

We specialize in custom streetwear, limited edition drops, and collaborations with up-and-coming designers. Our mission is to bring fresh drip straight to the streets. A variety of services are available from Slime Apparel that are designed to satisfy the demands of our target market. Designing and selling premium streetwear apparel, such as t-shirts, hoodies, and accessories that capture contemporary urban culture, is our main offering. Furthermore, we provide customization choices like printing and embroidery so that clients may make their clothing uniquely their own. In order to produce limited-edition drops that enhance our brand identity, we also collaborate with artists and influencers. We offer a smooth online purchase process with dependable countrywide shipping to make our items more accessible.
